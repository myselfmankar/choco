### [Assignment 8] Student RMS: Database Guide
---
Access the MongoDB Shell:
> mongosh

1. Switch to Student Database:
   use student_db

2. List All Student Records:
   db.students.find().pretty()

3. Filter: Find students with marks > 75 (Distinction):
   db.students.find({marks: {$gt: 75}}).pretty()

4. Filter: Search for a specific student by Name (Regex):
   db.students.find({name: /Donald/}).pretty()

5. Filter: Find by Roll Number:
   db.students.find({roll: "101"}).pretty()

6. Sort by Marks (Descending):
   db.students.find().sort({marks: -1}).pretty()

7. Manual Insert (Emergency Backup):
   db.students.insertOne({
     name: "Barack Obama",
     roll: "44",
     email: "barack@whitehouse.gov",
     marks: 98
   })
