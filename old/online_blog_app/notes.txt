### [Assignment 7] Online Blog: Database Guide
---
Access the MongoDB Shell:
> mongosh

1. Switch to Blog Database:
   use blog_db

2. List All Blog Posts:
   db.posts.find().pretty()

3. Filter: Find posts by a specific Author:
   db.posts.find({author: "Contributor"}).pretty()

4. Filter: Find posts containing "Insight" in the title (Regex):
   db.posts.find({title: /Insight/}).pretty()

5. Count total posts:
   db.posts.countDocuments()

6. Manual Insert (Emergency Backup):
   db.posts.insertOne({
     title: "Manual Entry",
     content: "Directly inserted via shell",
     author: "SysAdmin",
     date: new Date()
   })
