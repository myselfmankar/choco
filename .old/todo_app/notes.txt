### [Assignment 15] Task Dashboard: Database Guide
---
Access the MongoDB Shell:
> mongosh

1. Switch to To-Do Database:
   use todo_db

2. List All Tasks:
   db.tasks.find().pretty()

3. Filter: Find High Priority tasks:
   db.tasks.find({priority: "High"}).pretty()

4. Filter: Find Work-related tasks:
   db.tasks.find({category: "Work"}).pretty()

5. Filter: Find completed tasks:
   db.tasks.find({completed: true}).pretty()

6. Manual Insert (Emergency Backup):
   db.tasks.insertOne({
     text: "Submit LP2 Assignments",
     category: "Urgent",
     priority: "High",
     completed: false
   })
